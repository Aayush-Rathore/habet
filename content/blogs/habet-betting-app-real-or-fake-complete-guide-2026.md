### How Fraudsters Clone Legitimate Apps

Understanding how fake apps work helps you avoid them:

**Step 1: Clone the interface**
Fraudsters copy the visual design of legitimate apps like HABET, creating near-identical interfaces that fool casual users.

**Step 2: Create fake reviews**
They generate hundreds of fake positive reviews on unofficial app stores and social media to build false credibility.

**Step 3: Offer unrealistic bonuses**
Fake apps offer "500% welcome bonuses" or "guaranteed winning tips" to attract deposits.

**Step 4: Accept deposits, block withdrawals**
Once users deposit, withdrawal requests are rejected with excuses like "verification pending" or "account under review."

**Step 5: Disappear**
After collecting enough deposits, the fake app disappears — the website goes offline, customer support stops responding, and the money is gone.
