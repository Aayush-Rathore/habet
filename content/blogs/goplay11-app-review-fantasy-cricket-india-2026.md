### Scoring System

GoPlay11 uses a standard fantasy cricket scoring system:

**Batting:**
- Run: +1 point
- Boundary (4): +1 bonus point
- Six: +2 bonus points
- Half-century (50 runs): +8 bonus points
- Century (100 runs): +16 bonus points
- Duck (0 runs, dismissed): -2 points

**Bowling:**
- Wicket: +25 points
- Maiden over: +8 points
- 3-wicket haul: +4 bonus points
- 4-wicket haul: +8 bonus points
- 5-wicket haul: +16 bonus points

**Fielding:**
- Catch: +8 points
- Stumping: +12 points
- Run-out (direct): +12 points
- Run-out (indirect): +6 points

Understanding this scoring system is essential for making good captaincy decisions. A batsman who scores 80 runs with 4 sixes earns 80 + 8 (boundaries) + 8 (sixes) + 8 (half-century) = 104 points. A bowler who takes 3 wickets earns 75 + 4 (bonus) = 79 points. Batsmen generally score more fantasy points in T20 matches; bowlers score more in low-scoring games.
