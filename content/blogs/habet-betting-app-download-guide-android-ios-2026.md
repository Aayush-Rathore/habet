Android blocks APK installations from outside the Play Store by default. You need to enable this setting first.

**For Android 8.0 and above:**

2. Go to **Apps & Notifications** (or **Apps**)
3. Tap **Special App Access**
4. Select **Install Unknown Apps**
5. Find your browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Samsung Internet)
6. Toggle **Allow from this source** to ON

**For Android 7.0 and below:**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Go to **Security**
3. Find **Unknown Sources**
