## KYC Verification Guide {#kyc}

### Why KYC is Required

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is mandatory before your first withdrawal. This is a regulatory requirement that:

- Verifies your identity to prevent fraud
- Ensures you are 18+ years old
- Confirms your payment methods belong to you
- Protects your account from unauthorized access

KYC is a one-time process. Once verified, you don't need to repeat it unless your documents expire.

### Documents Required

**Primary ID (choose one):**
- Aadhaar card (front and back)
- PAN card (front only)
- Passport (photo page)
- Voter ID (front and back)
- Driving license (front and back)

**Proof of address (if not included in primary ID):**
- Utility bill (electricity, water, gas) — not older than 3 months
- Bank statement — not older than 3 months
- Aadhaar card (if address is current)

**Selfie:**
- Clear photo of your face
- Must match the photo on your ID document
- No sunglasses, hats, or face coverings

### Step-by-Step KYC Process

1. Open the [HABET APK](/) and go to **Account → Verify Identity**
2. Select your document type from the dropdown
3. Tap **Upload Front** and take a clear photo of the front of your document
4. Tap **Upload Back** (if required for your document type)
5. Tap **Take Selfie** and follow the on-screen instructions
6. Review all uploaded documents for clarity
7. Tap **Submit for Verification**

**KYC processing time:**
- Standard: 2-24 hours
- Peak periods (IPL season): Up to 48 hours
- You'll receive an SMS and email when KYC is approved

### Tips for Faster KYC Approval

- **Use good lighting:** Natural daylight produces the clearest photos
- **Avoid glare:** Don't photograph documents under direct light
- **Ensure all text is readable:** Zoom in to verify before submitting
- **Match your selfie to your ID:** Same hairstyle and no accessories
- **Submit during business hours:** 9 AM - 6 PM IST for faster processing
- **Use Aadhaar if possible:** Aadhaar verification is typically fastest

### KYC Rejection Reasons

If your KYC is rejected, common reasons include:

- **Blurry documents:** Retake photos with better lighting and stability
- **Expired documents:** Submit current, valid documents
- **Name mismatch:** Ensure your HABET account name matches your ID exactly
- **Partial document visible:** Ensure the entire document is in frame
- **Selfie mismatch:** Ensure your selfie clearly matches your ID photo

You can resubmit KYC documents after rejection. Contact customer support if you need guidance.

## Common Login Issues and Solutions {#troubleshooting}

### Issue 1: Incorrect Password

**Symptoms:** "Invalid password" error message

**Solutions:**
- Check Caps Lock is not enabled
- Try OTP login instead
- Reset your password via the Forgot Password option
- Ensure you're using the correct mobile number

### Issue 2: OTP Not Received

**Symptoms:** No SMS received after requesting OTP

**Solutions:**
- Wait 60 seconds before requesting a new OTP
- Check if your mobile number is correct
- Ensure your phone has network coverage
- Check if SMS is blocked by your carrier (some carriers block promotional SMS)
- Try requesting OTP via email instead

### Issue 3: Account Locked

**Symptoms:** "Account locked" message after multiple failed login attempts

**Solutions:**
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock after 5 failed attempts
- Contact customer support for immediate unlock
- Verify your identity to unlock the account

### Issue 4: App Not Loading After Login

**Symptoms:** App freezes or shows blank screen after login

**Solutions:**
- Force close the app and reopen
- Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → HABET → Clear Cache
- Check your internet connection
- Uninstall and reinstall the app (your account data is stored on HABET's servers)

### Issue 5: "Session Expired" Error

**Symptoms:** Logged out automatically with "Session Expired" message

**Solutions:**
- This is normal after 30 minutes of inactivity
- Log in again using your credentials or biometric login
- Enable "Remember Me" to extend session duration to 30 days
