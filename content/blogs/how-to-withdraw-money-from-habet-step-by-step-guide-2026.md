## Before You Withdraw: Prerequisites {#prerequisites}

### KYC Verification is Mandatory

HABET requires KYC (Know Your Customer) verification before processing your first withdrawal. This is a regulatory requirement, not optional.

**Required documents:**
- **Photo ID:** Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport
- **Proof of address:** Utility bill, bank statement, or Aadhaar (if address is included)
- **Selfie:** For facial verification against your ID

**KYC completion steps:**
1. Open the [HABET APK](/) and go to **Account → Verify Identity**
2. Select your document type
3. Upload clear photos of front and back of your ID
4. Take a selfie as instructed
5. Submit and wait for approval (typically 2-24 hours)

**Tips for faster KYC approval:**
- Ensure documents are clearly visible with no glare or blur
- Use good lighting — natural daylight works best
- Ensure the selfie clearly shows your face matching the ID photo
- Submit during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM IST) for faster processing

### Minimum Balance Requirement

You must have at least ₹500 in your main wallet to initiate a withdrawal. Bonus wallet funds cannot be withdrawn until wagering requirements are met.

### Verified Payment Method

Your withdrawal method must be verified and match your KYC documents. For UPI withdrawals, the UPI ID must be registered to the same name as your HABET account.

## UPI Withdrawal Guide {#upi}

UPI is the fastest and most popular withdrawal method for Indian HABET users. Processing time: 4-24 hours.

### Step-by-Step UPI Withdrawal

**Step 1: Open the HABET app**
Launch the [HABET APK](/) and log in to your account.

**Step 2: Navigate to Withdrawal**
- Tap the **Account** icon (bottom right)
- Select **Wallet**
- Tap **Withdraw**

**Step 3: Select UPI**
- Choose **UPI** from the withdrawal method list
- If this is your first UPI withdrawal, tap **Add UPI ID**

**Step 4: Add or Select Your UPI ID**
- Enter your UPI ID (e.g., yourname@upi, yourphone@paytm, yourphone@ybl)
- Verify the name associated with the UPI ID matches your HABET account name
- Tap **Verify UPI ID**

**Step 5: Enter Withdrawal Amount**
- Enter the amount you wish to withdraw (minimum ₹500)
- The available balance is shown above the input field
- Tap **Continue**

**Step 6: Confirm the Withdrawal**
- Review the withdrawal details:
  - Amount: ₹X,XXX
  - Method: UPI
  - UPI ID: yourname@upi
  - Processing time: 4-24 hours
- Tap **Confirm Withdrawal**

**Step 7: OTP Verification**
- Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number
- Tap **Submit**

**Step 8: Track Your Withdrawal**
- Go to **Account → Transaction History**
- Your withdrawal will show as "Pending" initially
- Status updates to "Processing" when HABET initiates the transfer
- Status updates to "Completed" when funds reach your UPI account

## Bank Transfer Withdrawal Guide {#bank-transfer}

Bank transfers are suitable for larger withdrawals or when UPI is unavailable. Processing time: 24-48 hours.

### Step-by-Step Bank Transfer Withdrawal

**Step 1: Add Your Bank Account**
Before your first bank transfer withdrawal:
1. Go to **Account → Payment Methods → Add Bank Account**
2. Enter your bank details:
   - Account holder name (must match KYC name)
   - Account number
   - IFSC code
   - Bank name and branch
3. HABET will send a small verification amount (₹1-₹5) to confirm the account
4. Enter the verification amount in the app to confirm

**Step 2: Initiate Withdrawal**
1. Go to **Account → Wallet → Withdraw**
2. Select **Bank Transfer**
3. Choose your verified bank account
4. Enter the withdrawal amount (minimum ₹500)
5. Tap **Continue**

**Step 3: Confirm and Submit**
1. Review the withdrawal details
2. Enter the OTP sent to your mobile
3. Tap **Confirm Withdrawal**

**Step 4: Track Status**
Bank transfers typically show as "Processing" for 24-48 hours before completing. You'll receive a push notification when the transfer is complete.

### NEFT vs IMPS

HABET uses IMPS (Immediate Payment Service) for bank transfers, which is faster than NEFT:
- **IMPS:** Available 24/7, transfers complete within 30 minutes to 2 hours after HABET processes the request
- **NEFT:** Available during banking hours, transfers complete within 2-4 hours

## Common Withdrawal Issues and Solutions {#issues}

### Issue 1: Withdrawal Rejected

**Cause:** Most common reasons for rejection:
- KYC not completed or documents expired
- Withdrawal method name doesn't match KYC name
- Active bonus with incomplete wagering requirements
- Suspicious activity flag on account

**Solution:**
1. Check your email for a rejection reason from HABET
2. Complete or update KYC if required
3. Ensure your payment method name matches your account name
4. Contact customer support via live chat for clarification

### Issue 2: Withdrawal Stuck in "Pending"

**Cause:** Pending status means HABET has received your request but hasn't processed it yet.

**Solution:**
- Wait for the standard processing time (4-24 hours for UPI)
- If still pending after 24 hours, contact customer support with your transaction ID
- Do not submit duplicate withdrawal requests

### Issue 3: Wrong UPI ID Entered

**Cause:** Typo in UPI ID or UPI ID belongs to a different person.

**Solution:**
- If the withdrawal hasn't been processed, contact customer support immediately to cancel
- If already processed to wrong UPI ID, contact support — recovery depends on the recipient's cooperation
- Always double-check UPI IDs before confirming

### Issue 4: Bank Account Verification Failed

**Cause:** Bank account details don't match KYC documents, or the verification amount wasn't entered correctly.

**Solution:**
1. Verify your account number and IFSC code are correct
2. Check your bank statement for the small verification amount (₹1-₹5)
3. Enter the exact amount in the verification field
4. If issues persist, contact customer support

### Issue 5: Withdrawal Limit Exceeded

**Cause:** Attempting to withdraw more than the daily or monthly limit.

**Solution:**
- Split the withdrawal into multiple transactions across different days
- Contact customer support to discuss VIP limit increases if you're a high-volume bettor

## Tax Considerations for Indian Bettors {#tax}

### TDS on Winnings

Under Indian tax law (Section 194B of the Income Tax Act), winnings from betting are subject to Tax Deducted at Source (TDS):

- **TDS rate:** 30% on net winnings above ₹10,000 per transaction
- **Who deducts:** HABET deducts TDS before processing withdrawals above the threshold
- **TDS certificate:** HABET provides Form 16A for TDS deducted

**Example:**
- You win ₹15,000 on a bet
- TDS deducted: ₹15,000 × 30% = ₹4,500
- Net withdrawal: ₹10,500

### Filing Your Tax Return

Betting winnings must be declared in your Income Tax Return under "Income from Other Sources." Consult a tax professional for personalized advice. HABET provides transaction history reports to help with tax filing.
