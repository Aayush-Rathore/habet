## Odds Comparison: Who Offers the Best Value? {#odds}

### Understanding Odds Margins

The odds margin (also called "vig" or "juice") is the percentage the bookmaker takes from every bet. Lower margins mean better value for bettors.

**Formula:** Margin = (1/Odds1 + 1/Odds2 - 1) × 100

For a coin flip with true 50/50 odds:
- Fair odds: 2.00 on both sides (0% margin)
- HABET typical odds: 1.95/1.95 (2.56% margin)
- Industry average: 1.87/1.87 (6.95% margin)
